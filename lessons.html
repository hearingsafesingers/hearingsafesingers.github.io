The approach

The Hearing-Safe Performance Method

Proprioceptive pitch awareness

We train your body to sense pitch through vibration and physical sensation — so you stay in tune regardless of what you can or can't hear.

Tinnitus management strategies

Practical techniques to reduce tinnitus interference before, during, and after singing, so difficult listening days don't have to mean silent ones.

Safe vocal production

Every exercise is calibrated to protect your voice and your hearing — no strain, no excessive volume, no guesswork.

Visual & tactile feedback

We use mirrors, touch, and visual cues as primary feedback tools so that hearing isn't a prerequisite for accurate, expressive singing.

Confidence under uncertainty

We build resilience and performance confidence so that unpredictable hearing days don't derail your progress or your joy in singing.

Bespoke from session one

No two hearing profiles are the same. Every lesson is adapted to your specific hearing loss, tinnitus, and vocal goals from the very first session.
