The teacher

A voice that guides
yours forward.

Hi, I'm Lili — a vocal coach who's passionate about helping singers navigate their careers with the uncertainty of their hearing condition.

As someone with tinnitus and hearing loss myself, I know first-hand the struggles and anxiety that come with trying to pursue a career in music. That lived experience shapes everything I do.

With a performance background spanning concert halls, recording studios, and theatre stages, my philosophy is simple: every voice is unique, and teaching should honour that. I work to understand your voice deeply before guiding it — never forcing, always nurturing.

Central to everything I do is protecting and nurturing your hearing. We work in ways that keep your ears safe — so you can keep singing for years to come, without fear of making things worse.

My values

What every lesson is built on.

Fun

Lessons should be enjoyable. I bring lightness and humour to every session — because when you're relaxed and having fun, your voice opens up in ways that serious drilling never achieves.

Respectful

Every student's experience, background, and hearing journey is unique. I meet you exactly where you are, without judgement, and at a pace that honours your process.

Peaceful

The studio is a safe space — free from pressure, comparison, or anxiety. We work calmly and patiently, building confidence through consistency rather than intensity.

Explorative

I encourage curiosity over perfection. We try things, discover what works for your voice and your hearing, and treat every session as an opportunity to learn something new about yourself as a singer.
