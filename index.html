About the teacher

Helping musicians with hearing loss find their voice — safely.

Singing with hearing loss and tinnitus was one of the hardest things I've ever faced. The uncertainty, the anxiety, the fear that performing might make things worse — it felt like the career I'd worked for was slipping away.

So I threw myself into research — studying the impact of hearing loss and tinnitus on the singing voice, and how the voice and body can be retrained to work around them. That research became the foundation of the Hearing-Safe Performance Method: a specialist approach that lets singers perform confidently and safely, whatever their hearing.
